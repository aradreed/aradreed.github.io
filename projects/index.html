Projects

Fall Ball. Currently only available for iOS, Fall Ball. is a game where the player tries to get a ball to the bottom of levels as quick as possible.
Size Squared A game developed at MHacks IV for iOS and Android. Tap the screen when the square sizes match to get points. Tap too early, lose a life. Tap too late, lose a life.
Up Down A simple game developed at YCHacks 2014 for the Pebble smartwatch. Press up or down when it says to score points.
First of Them A team developed game at YCHacks 2014 for the Pebble Smartwatch. Press a button to shoot zombies when the pointer goes over the colored space at the bottom.

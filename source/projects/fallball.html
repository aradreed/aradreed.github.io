Instead of controlling the ball through obstacles, you must interact with the obstacles to guide the ball to the bottom of the level as fast as possible.

Features
- Leaderboards
- Personal high scores
- Star ratings for levels
- Share score on Twitter

How to Play
- Swipe to slide wooden blocks
- Tap to destroy rock blocks
- Metal cannot be moved
- Hit the booster pads to speed up!
- Avoid the goo!
- Grab power-ups like ghost ball or timewarp
